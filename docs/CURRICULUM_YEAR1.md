# Curriculum outline – Year 1

**Audience:** Ages 8–12, absolute beginners.  
**Cadence:** 3–5 minute activities, 3–4 times per week.  
**Format:** Each unit ships as JSON lessons; each lesson includes 3–5 short activities.

## Q1 – Letter foundations
- Recognize isolated forms of the 28 letters.
- Match letter shapes to sounds (with simple visual mnemonics).
- Quick drills: tap the correct letter for a given sound.

## Q2 – Short vowels and basic syllables
- Introduce fatḥa, kasra, ḍamma on high-frequency letters.
- Build consonant+vowel syllables (CV) and simple open syllables.
- Activities: match sound to syllable, sort cards by vowel.

## Q3 – Simple words
- Blend 2–3 letter words with short vowels.
- Distinguish minimal pairs (بَ vs بُ).
- Activities: pick the word that matches a played audio cue (placeholder for now).

## Q4 – Reading fluency starters
- Timed recognition of familiar words.
- Light comprehension prompts with picture cues (non-text answers).
- Activities: choose the matching picture for a short written word.

## Pedagogical notes
- Keep instructions concise and visual; avoid free text.
- Repetition with variation: recycle target letters across lessons.
- Celebrate streaks lightly; enforce cooldowns on the companion to avoid over-stimulation.
- Track progress locally only (lesson completion + last activity index).
